Color Fall is a mobile game prototype developed in Unity using C#, inspired by Tetris. The player must catch the falling colored blocks and place them on the corresponding color slots on the floor.
The player needs to **catch falling colors** and place them on the corresponding color zones on the floor.  

---

## ðŸŽ® Gameplay

- **Objective:** Catch the falling colored cubes and place them correctly.  
- **Mechanics:**  
  - Cubes fall from the top of the screen.  
  - Tap or drag to move the cube to the matching color zone.
  - Rainbow cubes to increase the challenge.
  - Score points for correct placements.  
  - The game ends if any cube are misplaced.  

---

## ðŸ•¹ Controls

- **Touch screen:** Drag or tap to move cubes.  
- **Mobile-friendly:** Designed for vertical orientation (Full HD recommended: 1080x1920).
